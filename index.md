# PASRS Helper

A browser extension for Pokémon Showdown that auto-saves your battle replays and
fills a **PASRS Google Sheet** with stats parsed from each game — your picks,
leads, Terastallization, Elo, and opponent match-ups.

All processing happens in your browser; data goes only from your browser to your
own Google Sheet. There is no third-party server.

- **Source code:** <https://github.com/nimnim111/Pasrs-Helper-Sheets>
- **Privacy policy:** [PRIVACY](PRIVACY)
- **Terms of service:** [TERMS](TERMS)

## What it does

- Records and uploads your Showdown replays.
- Auto-creates a PASRS tracker spreadsheet in your Google Drive.
- Parses each recorded replay and writes the data into the tracker, so the
  template's dashboards (Game By Game, Usage, Matchups, Leads, Move Usage) fill
  in automatically.
- Lets you pick one of your Showdown teams to populate the Usage Stats page.
