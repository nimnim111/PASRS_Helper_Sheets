# PASRS Helper — Terms of Service

_Last updated: 2026-06-29_

PASRS Helper is a free, open-source browser extension for Pokémon Showdown that
records your battle replays and writes stats parsed from them into **your own**
PASRS Google Sheet. By installing or using the extension, you agree to these
terms.

## 1. The service

PASRS Helper runs entirely in your browser. It saves your Pokémon Showdown
replays, can create a PASRS tracker spreadsheet in your Google Drive, and writes
parsed game data into that spreadsheet. There is no server operated by the
developer; the extension communicates only with Pokémon Showdown and, after you
sign in, with Google's APIs on your behalf.

## 2. Your account and data

- You are responsible for your Google account and the data in your own
  spreadsheet. The extension only creates and edits the tracker file it makes
  (via the `drive.file` scope) and writes to that spreadsheet (via the
  `spreadsheets` scope).
- You can revoke the extension's access at any time at
  <https://myaccount.google.com/permissions>, and delete your tracker spreadsheet
  from Google Drive whenever you wish.
- Handling of your data is described in the
  [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY).
